Our Journey

Bannada Daara (The Colored Thread) began with a vision to bridge traditional craftsmanship with modern sustainable fashion in Dakshina Kannada.

We focus on zero-waste production, upcycling fabrics into premium treasures. Every stitch supports local artisans and eco-conscious design.
